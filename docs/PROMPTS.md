# Example Prompts

These are copy/paste prompts you can use in the CLI.

## Example 1 — Japan (multi-city, relaxed pace)

> I’m traveling to Japan. Flying from NYC. Dates: 2026-04-05 to 2026-04-14. Budget is $3500 for 2 travelers. Interests: ramen, gardens, museums, day hikes. Pace: relaxed. Please give flight + hotel search links, a day-by-day itinerary, transit notes, typical weather for those dates, a rough budget breakdown, and export an .ics calendar.

## Example 2 — Europe (validation + preferences)

> I want a 6-day trip to Lisbon, Portugal. Departing from SFO. Dates: 2026-05-12 to 2026-05-17. I’m traveling solo with a $1800 budget. I like walking tours, coffee shops, viewpoints, and seafood. Pace: balanced. Include flight/hotel links, neighborhoods to stay in, a day-by-day plan with morning/afternoon/evening blocks, transit tips, and an .ics export.

## Example 3 — Missing fields (will trigger ASK_USER)

> Plan a trip to Thailand. I like street food and beaches. Give me an itinerary and an .ics export.
